## What Went Wrong

They tried reinventing design, manufacturing, materials, and installation simultaneously. Each project surfaced new challenges with enormous capital requirements.

> "Construction punishes the unprepared."

Filed bankruptcy early 2024 after completing a fraction of its pipeline.
