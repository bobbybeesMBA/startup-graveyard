Olive AI was the "AI workforce for healthcare" — automating admin tasks. Valued at $4B with 800+ employees at peak.

## What Went Wrong

Rather than perfecting one workflow, Olive expanded across revenue cycle, clinical, and supply chain — each a complex domain with different buyers and regulations.

> "Hospitals signed up for magic and got middleware."

Wound down late 2023, selling off units after burning through most funding.
